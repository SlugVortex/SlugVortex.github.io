Who I Am

I am a final-year Computer Science student at Northern Caribbean University, Jamaica, finishing up in August 2026. My work sits at the intersection of backend engineering, LLM integration, and Azure cloud architecture — the parts of AI products that actually have to work under pressure.

At the Microsoft Innovation Challenge 2025, I placed 2nd out of 70+ teams with CivicUtopia and 3rd out of 40+ teams with ArtemisShield — two separate projects, same month. I hold the Microsoft Azure AI Engineer Associate (AI-102) certification, which most people in industry don't have, let alone students still in university.

I am from Spaldings, Manchester — a small town in the Jamaican highlands. That background gives me a particular relationship with figuring things out without a lot of resources. It shows up in how I write code: deliberate, performant, maintainable. Open to relocation anywhere.
